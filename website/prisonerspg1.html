Welcome to Prisoner Number One

Instructions:

  • Welcome to Prisoner's Dilema

  • The main goal of this game is to know "How well do you know your opponent or how well your opponent knows you?"

  • In order to play this game you will need to enter your name, age, and gender.

  • Please click on the Start button to get started.

Rules:

  • If each player confesses, each of them will serve 2 years in prison.

  • If one player confesses and the other remains silent, the player who confessed will be set free and the player who remained silent serves 3 years.

  • If both player remain silent, both will serve 1 years.
